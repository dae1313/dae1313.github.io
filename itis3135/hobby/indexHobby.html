Foods

This is my top 3 foods:

This is a vietnamese dish. It is called Banh Beo which is similar to rice cakes. It has a small, white, round shape with a chewy texture. People can add various toppings to it, such as minced shrimp, pork, green onions, and/or fried shallots. When eating banh beo, it's better to eat it with a dipping sauce, like fish sauce.

My other favorite dish is the Banh Cuon which is a vietnamese rice rolls. The rolls are very thin sheets made out of rice flour. People can fill the rolls with ground pork, minced shallots, and mushrooms. This dish goes well with fish sauce, giving it a unique taste.

This is Korean BBQ, which is the best. In a restaurant, it includes raw meats such as beef, chicken, or pork. They also provide various side dishes to accompany the meat. I like Korean BBQ because I can cook it the way I like and eat as much as I want, especially in an all-you-can-eat restaurant.

Future

How I pick a place to travel:

I like to do my research and set a price range so I don't spend more than intended. I look up the scenery and ratings to see if it's worth visiting. Then, I try to pick a date based on the weather and any fun events happening. Sometimes, if it's not a super long distance, I prefer to drive. For example, if I'm going to a different state, I enjoy seeing the views while driving. I like to explore and see what's there. In the future, I want to travel to Paris, Australia, South Korea, and Hawaii.
